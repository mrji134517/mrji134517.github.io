About Our School

Welcome to AoE High School—where faith drives purpose and learning has no limits. We challenge minds, shape character, and build leaders who stand strong in intellect, conviction, and heart. As a Christ-centered school, we believe education is more than knowledge—it's about impact. Here, students grow in wisdom, service, and leadership in a dynamic, in-person environment. Every student is uniquely gifted by God, and we’re here to help them step into their calling with confidence.
